Rust Mini Server Information

Rust Mini is a small monthly wipe 'vanilla-ish' Rust server designed for players who work, have families, or simply prefer a more relaxed progression pace still with the threat of pvp & raiding. The server will have slight changes month to month until polished. You will also notice subtle 'Quality of Life' tweaks, it's up to you to find them.

Raiding

To focus more on PvP and Online Raiding, players are limited to 1x offline raid every 24 hrs. When you start an offline raid your UI will trigger to 1/1. You will be locked out of any other offline raiding for 24hrs.

This setting also helps to prevent people from being online 24/7 raiding the entire server while players are at work.

Farms

To prevent massive horse farms producing massive amounts of fertilizer to trade for scrap and get players out of their bases and play instead, horses will not spawn in the wild, you will have to buy them and you will find they poo less but enough to use fertilizer as intended, in planter boxes.

Fish farms have a slight boost in profit when sold at fishing villages.

Chicken farms, sell your surplus eggs! This will give RP's something else to do.

Events

Vanilla event times are normal.

Addition events, Plane Crash & Monument Bradley are dynamic between 1 to 3 hr spawn times.
