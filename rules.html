Rules

  • Two players to a team maximium
  • Do not roam with non team mates
  • No overly toxic chat
  • No impersonating other players or admin
  • Do not advertise other servers
  • Only call admin if urgent using /helpme in chat

    If admin is not available, they will leave a note in your inventory or contact you via steam

WARNING!

Cheaters will have all evidence compiled. Any video recording will be uploaded to public youtube with a detailed report and link to video provided to facepunch devs.

Reporting Players

There a options to report ( hell, use both ).

1. Always use ingame F7 to report suspected cheaters, this is most important and admin will also receive a copy of your report.

2. Want to add more info? Use ingame chat command /complain to include your report & video or image url of cheaters or problems.

* Unfortunately Rust still has a massive cheater problem with hackers going undetected by facepunch. To combat this, there are tools & ways for admins to weed out possible hackers remotely, out of game & ingame. There may be an active admin on and there are several non active admins monitoring this server.

PLEASE DO NOT CALL OUT HACKS IN GAME! JUST REPORT! SAY NOTHING!
