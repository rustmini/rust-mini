FAQ's

Why are the maps small?

If you enjoy vanilla Rust, you've probably experienced the same problem on many 100-200 population servers. The wipe starts strong, but within a couple of days the population drops dramatically, leaving only a handful of players spread across a huge map.

With every monument available and multiple locations to obtain Blue and Red Keycards, players rarely cross paths. The world feels empty, player interaction becomes uncommon, and PvP encounters become increasingly rare.

Why is there no Launch Site or Giant Excavator?

There is simply no room for these monuments on small maps. The only way around this is to produce a custom map for each wipe that runs the risk of having glitches that cannot be fixed ( example: floating monuments ) without wiping the server again after facepunch forced update.

For your pew pewing pleasure, the Bradley APC spawns at main monuments!
