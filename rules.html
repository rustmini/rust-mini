Rules

  • Two players to a team maximium
  • Do not roam with non team mates
  • No overly toxic chat
  • No impersonating other players or admin
  • Do not advertise other servers
  • Only call admin if urgent using /helpme in chat

    If admin is not available, they will leave a note in your inventory or contact you via steam

WARNING!

Any player found cheating will have all available evidence thoroughly documented and compiled. Where applicable, video recordings of the offence will be uploaded to a publicly accessible YouTube channel and accompanied by a detailed incident report. A direct link to the video evidence, along with all relevant supporting information, will be submitted to the Facepunch development team for further review and investigation. Our goal is to maintain a fair and enjoyable environment for all players, and we take all forms of cheating and exploitative behaviour seriously.

Reporting Players

There are options to report.

1. If you suspect a player of cheating, always submit a report using the in-game F7 reporting system. This is the most effective way to ensure that reports are properly logged and reviewed by Facepunch. Reports submitted through the F7 system are also forwarded to server administrators, allowing us to investigate incidents more efficiently. When reporting a player, please provide as much detail as possible, including the player's name, location, and a description of the suspicious behaviour.

2. Want to add more info? Use ingame chat command /helpme to include your report & video or image url of cheaters or problems.

* Unfortunately, cheating remains an ongoing challenge within the Rust community, and not all malicious activity is immediately detected by automated anti-cheat systems. To help maintain a fair and enjoyable environment, our administration team utilizes a range of tools and investigative methods to identify and monitor suspicious behaviour. These tools allow administrators to review player activity both in-game and remotely, enabling effective oversight even when staff are not actively connected to the server. While an administrator may occasionally be online and visible, please be aware that several members of our staff team regularly monitor server activity behind the scenes. As a result, administrative oversight may be occurring even when no admins appear to be present in-game.
