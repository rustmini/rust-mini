Custom Events Information

Plane Crash

A Cargo Plane has engine trouble! If it crashes, surviving scientists will protect the loot for 20 minutes.

Monument Bradley

Small maps cannot have Launch Site, so Bradley will spawn at high tier monuments with a 2hr cooldown.

Naval Scientists

Naval Scientists from the Deep Sea stray closer to the Island! Take their loot! Careful! They have booby trapped the RHIB! 1-2hr cooldown.
