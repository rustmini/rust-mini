Server Rules

  1. Solo or Duo Only.
  2. No roaming with non team mates.
  3. Respect all players, do not be toxic.
  4. No cheating, scripting, or exploiting.
  5. No hate speech or targeted harassment.
  6. Do not impersonate staff.
  7. Unhappy? Do not trash talk the server, just leave.
  8. No advertising other servers
  9. Admin decisions are final.
  10. Trying to work around the rules will see you perma banned.
  11. The admin is not your friend, do not ask for favours.
  12. Do not call out hacks in game, just report, very important.

Reporting Players

There a options to report.

1. Use ingame F7 to report suspected cheaters, this is most important and admin will also receive a copy of your report.

2. Use ingame chat command /complain to include your text, video or image url of cheaters or problems.

* Unfortunately Rust still has a massive cheater problem with hackers going undetected by facepunch. To combat this, there are tools & ways for admins to weed out possible hackers remotely, out of game & ingame. There may be an active admin on and there are several non active admins monitoring this server.
