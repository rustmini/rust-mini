About RUST Mini

Designed to focus on PvP and online raiding, with less emphasis on offline raiding and overnight event farming when not enough players are available to contest objectives. Smaller maps are carefully selected to improve performance and player engagement, ensuring you're not stuck on a massive map with only a handful of players for the remainder of the wipe.

The best loot won't come easy. If you want the top-tier rewards, be prepared to battle for control of the Oil Rigs, Patrol Helicopter, and Bradley APC.
