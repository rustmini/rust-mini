Server Rules

  • Solo or Duo Only.
  • No roaming with non team mates.
  • Respect all players, do not be toxic.
  • No cheating, scripting, or exploiting.
  • No hate speech or targeted harassment.
  • Do not impersonate staff.
  • Unhappy? Do not trash talk the server, just leave.
  • No advertising other servers.
  • Trying to work around the rules will see you perma banned.
  • The admin is not your friend, do not ask for favours.
  • Do not call out hacks in game, just report, very important.

    • Any player caught hacking, will be named and shamed with video evidence uploaded to youtube with steam i.d posted to facepunch discord report.

    Reporting Players

    There a options to report ( hell, use both ).

    1. Always use ingame F7 to report suspected cheaters, this is most important and admin will also receive a copy of your report.

    2. Want to add more info? Use ingame chat command /complain to include your report & video or image url of cheaters or problems.
