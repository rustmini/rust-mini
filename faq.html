FAQ's

Why are the maps small?

If you enjoy playing a vanilla server, you have more than likely found it to be a 100 or 200 pop server, and after two days there is only a dozen people on a huge map with every single monument on the island with multiple ways to get blue and red cards, you never bump into another player, pvp becomes very rare.

Why is there no launch site or Giant Excavatr?

There is simply no room for these monuments on small maps. The only way around this is to produce a custom map for each wipe that runs the risk of having glitches that cannot be fixed without wiping the server again after facepunch hotfix releases.
