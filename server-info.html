Raiding

To focus more on PvP and Online Raiding, players are limited to 1x offline raid every 24 hrs. When you start an offline raid your UI will trigger to 1/1 on the bottom right of your screen. You will be locked out of any other offline raiding for 24hrs.

This setting also helps to prevent people from being online 24/7 raiding the entire server while players are at work.

Events

Vanilla event times are normal.
