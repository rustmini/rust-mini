Rust Mini Server Information

Rust Mini is a monthly wipe Solo/Duo server created by a couple of Rust enthusiasts for players who have jobs, families, and no desire to play on 200-pop battlefield servers or spend the rest of the wipe wandering a giant map populated by eight survivors.

Enjoy a mostly vanilla Rust experience with a relaxed progression pace, balanced by the constant threat of PvP, raids, and poor decision-making. High-tier military loot isn't available around every corner. If you want the good stuff, you'll need to fight for it at the Oil Rigs, Patrol Helicopter, and Bradley APC.

Outpost & Bandit Camp are merged with Mini's available for purchase on the roof. Maps are kept small to keep players engaged.

Raiding

To focus more on PvP and Online Raiding, players are limited to 1x offline raid every 24 hrs. When you start an offline raid your UI will trigger to 1/1 on the bottom right of your screen. You will be locked out of any other offline raiding for 24hrs.

This setting also helps to prevent people from being online 24/7 raiding the entire server while players are at work.

The previous rule of no griefing has been lifted. If you raid a base, you can now take the base over. Use it, seal it and let it decay it's up to you and just treated as game play.

Events

Vanilla event times are normal.

Additional Custom Events, Plane Crash & Monument Bradley are dynamic between 1 to 3 hr spawn times.

Admins

There are no playing admins or moderators on this server — none, zero.

Any connected admin is strictly here for maintenance & monitoring. Admins are adults & permanently invisible in-game, do not play and do not engage with players.

If you need assistance, use the in-game commands:

• /complain • /suggest

*Example: /complain I think Mr Player is cheating please check!

Messages sent through these commands are reviewed as soon as available.
