Server Rules

  1. Respect all players, do not be toxic.
  2. No cheating, scripting, or exploiting.
  3. No hate speech or targeted harassment.
  4. Do not impersonate staff.
  5. Admin decisions are final.
  6. Trying to work around the rules will see you perma banned.

Reporting Players

There a options to report.

1. Use the ingame F7 to report suspected cheaters, this is most important and admin will also receive a copy of your report.

2. Use chat command /complain to include your text, video or image url of cheaters or problems.
